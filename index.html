About

Welcome to Becca Cooks - Preparing Ghanaian dishes the African way!

Our approach to cooking is inventive backed by our west African heritage, we skillfully serve our customers with indiginous Ghanaian dishes as well as other innovative dishes that combine the finest seasonal Ghanaian produce with specially sourced african spices. We cater for all events; big and small and individual takeouts.
